FISHING

INSTAGRAM

Fishing is the activity of trying to catch fish. Fish are often caught in the wild
but may also be caught from stocked bodies of water.
Techniques for catching fish include hand gathering, spearing, netting, angling and trapping.
"Fishing" may include catching aquatic animals other than fish, such as molluscs, crustaceans,
and echinoderms (such as starfish and sea urchins).
